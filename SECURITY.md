 **About Me & Project Purpose**

I am a **recent graduate** with an Associate Degree in **Cybersecurity and Information Technology – Networking**. This project, **MegaCyberLab**, was created to demonstrate my **hands-on skills in cybersecurity, networking, and lab management**.

The purpose of this project is to:

* **Showcase practical knowledge** of offensive and defensive security techniques.
* Provide a **safe, simulated environment** for learning penetration testing, incident monitoring, and SOC operations.
* Illustrate the **Principle of Least Privilege, role-based access control, and other security concepts** through lab simulations.
* Serve as a **portfolio-ready project** for potential employers, highlighting my ability to design, implement, and document cybersecurity lab environments.

By building MegaCyberLab, I aim to **bridge the gap between theoretical learning and practical experience**, demonstrating that I can create fully functional, interactive cybersecurity solutions using Python and JSON-based lab configurations.
